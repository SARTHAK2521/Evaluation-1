Our Story

Start Great, Start Out of the Ordinary!

LA Americana Gourmet is a brand that comes with a thoughtful expression of modern craft through its novel innovation in the form of an authentic gourmet bakery, which is undoubtedly Out of the Ordinary. Our exclusively curated products come with No Added Preservatives and Longer Shelf Life. Try our gourmet range of bakery products for pure indulgence.

How Are We Different?

  • Carefully Selected Quality Ingredients: All the LA Americana Gourmet products are baked with the finest quality ingredients including organic oats, wheat flour, garlic, fresh fruits, and other natural sources of raw materials without any added preservatives.
  • Freshly Baked with Love: Our recipes are easy and simple and all the products are baked with love ensuring the final product is liked by all. Our products remain fresh for a longer time.
  • Packed with Care: All our products are packed thoughtfully to ensure their safety and freshness until you bite into it. Product packs also contain all the details regarding the ingredients and other specifications for easy consumption.
  • Authentic Taste of Gourmet: Taste is what we have never compromised on. So, every product in our gourmet range is irresistibly delicious and made without using too much sugar, refined flour, or fat.
